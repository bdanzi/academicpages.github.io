About me

I am a Master Student in Particle Physics at the Physics Department of the Bari University Aldo Moro. My research and teaching focus on Experimental Particle Physics.

I am currently working on a data analysis project about signal discrimination of Higgs production via vector boson fusion (VBF) vs background using Google Colab. It will be propose to High School students in an INFN hackathon.

Master thesis: quite soon,stay tuned!
