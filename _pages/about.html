About me

I am a Master Student in Particle Physics at the Physics Department of the Bari University Aldo Moro. My research and studies focus on Experimental Particle Physics.

I am currently working on a data analysis project to students about discrimination of Higgs production via vector boson fusion (VBF) signal vs background using Google's Colaboratory. It will be proposed to High School students in an INFN hackathon.

Master thesis: quite soon,stay tuned!
